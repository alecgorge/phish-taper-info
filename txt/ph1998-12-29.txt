Tech Notes

FLAC/SHNs decoded and Wavemerge was used to reassemble the entire 
show back to 1 long wave.  CEP2.0 was used to align & synch all 
of the sources.  CEP2.0 was used for the multitrack mixdown.  
This is a 60%-SBD, 25%-AUD1, 15%-AUD2 mix.

- FLAC conversion done 26-OCT-2008 (3 source mix)
- SHN conversion done 11-MAR-2005 (2 source mix)
- Artwork at 300dpi by dan@am-dig.com

photo paper.  Design for use in a 2CD clear, slim CD case.

Notes

Entire show is seamless.

d1t01 "Rock and Roll" the SBD source started very abruptly.  This
was fixed with a lead in patch from the AUD sources and a crossfade
to the SBD source.

d1t07 "Brian and Robert" tape flip on the SBD source after the song.  
Patched and crossfaded with the AUD sources and is no longer inaudible.

d1t11 "Free" the SBD source started very abruptly.  This was fixed 
with a lead in patch from the AUD sources and a crossfade to the SBD 
source.

d2t03 "Boogie On Reggae Woman" tape flip on the SBD source after the 
song.  Patched and crossfaded with the AUD sources and is no longer 
inaudible.

d2t05 "Encore Break" the SBD source was missing a minute or so of the
crowd cheer prior to the encore.  Fixed with a patch from the AUD 
sources and a crossfade to the SBD source.

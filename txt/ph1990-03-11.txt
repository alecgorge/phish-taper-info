Notes on source #1
-The Phish Companion does not list d1t11 Lizards for this show but it is definitely 
  part of the show since they are no cuts before and there is a nice segue into the 
  song.
-This is the first time this show exists digitally and is the most complete source  
  with a known cassette generation.
-Tape flips
  d1t11 8:03 (38:56)
  d2t05 2:15 (46:49)
   **From the second tape flip, it seems that the supposed first 6 songs of the second 
     set were not recorded.  
-Levels slighly high at start of d2t01 but are lowered eventually.

Notes on source #2

The first few seconds of Carolina were trashed due to tape rot. The end of Bowie was cut, so I added a fade out. 
The quality of the patch source was comparable to the original shn filesets, however the exact linage of the analog used is
not known. Hopefully this will be considered a more complete upgrade, especially considering the inclusion of the missing songs, 
especially the ultra rare Roll Like A Canteloupe. Again - huge thanks to Greg Peeler for digging this up!
Enjoy! Any questions, feel free to email me.

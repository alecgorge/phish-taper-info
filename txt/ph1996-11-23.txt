Notes:
The neumann source is nearly complete missing only the encore. It was a shorten fileset. The encore
was an unknown source and encoded to flac. To create one complete recording using only one type 
of encoding I converted everything to wav then to flac. The only alteration I made was to clip about
18 seconds off of the end of Hood using CDwave. There was nothing there, it fades out at 15:32 and had nothing
but silence until 15:50. When the unknown source for GTBT kicks in is it obvious that there is gap 
between the end of the first recording and the beginning of the second. The unknown recording kicks in as the boys
are coming back on stage for the encore, no music is lost, just some cheering. The second source is a little louder than 
the first but both are solid. Sonically I did nothing to alter these sources. This is a fantastic show and 
great recording, enjoy!! -disco

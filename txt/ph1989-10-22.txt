Show Notes: Suzy included Purple Haze teases from Trey. SOAM included an extended drum solo from Fishman. Fishman explained some of his vacuum parts before launching into a vacuum solo during I Didn't Know. Members of the Ninja Custodians visited the stage before and during the encore, leading to some funny stage banter. Undun was subsequently dedicated to Sofi (likely Sofi Dillof) on her 22nd birthday and the lyrics to the song were changed to reference the event and the Ninja Custodians' hijinks.

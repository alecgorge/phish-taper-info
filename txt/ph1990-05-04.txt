Just for the record; There has been some confusion and debate among people concerning the order
of these sets because of a comment Trey makes after Whipping Post ("Welcome to the second set, sorry
we took such a long break..."). It was meant as a joke. At the Keene State College Show prior to this
one, the band was not allowed to play a second set because of a fire alarm activation due to people smoking
in the building, which was subsequently closed after the first set. The Fire Marshall was pissed. Having attended both that show and this one, I'm here to tell you quite indisputably That the set/song order listed here is
correct. Also, Highway to Hell ended the second set. The band left the stage, then came back out and
played a double encore.
